Probability of ectopic pregnancy
An ectopic pregnancy occurs when the fertilized egg attaches itself in a place other than inside the uterus. Almost all ectopic pregnancies occur in the fallopian tube and are thus sometimes called tubal pregnancies. ... An ectopic pregnancy happens in 1 out of 50 pregnancies.
