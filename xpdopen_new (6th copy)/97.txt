What is the prognosis for breast cancer?
Chances for survival vary by stage of breast cancer. ... Cancer that has not spread beyond the breast has a better prognosis than cancer that has spread to the lymph nodes. The poorest prognosis is for metastatic breast cancer (stage IV), when the cancer has spread beyond the lymph nodes to other parts of the body.
