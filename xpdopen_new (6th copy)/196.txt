Chances of miscarriage
With one past miscarriage, the odds of miscarrying in your next pregnancy are about 20 percent. This is not much higher than someone without a history of miscarriage. With two previous miscarriages, the risk of another miscarriage is 28 percent, and with three previous miscarriages, the risk increases to 43 percent.6 days ago
