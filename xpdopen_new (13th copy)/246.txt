10 weeks pregnant chance of miscarriage
Random chromosomal error causes 70 percent of pregnancies that end before six weeks of gestation, 50 percent of pregnancies that stop between six and 10 weeks and only 5 percent of pregnancies that end after 10 weeks. Women cannot do much about some risk factors, such as previous miscarriages and advanced maternal age.Jan 27, 2008
