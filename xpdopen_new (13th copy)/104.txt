What are the odds of becoming a professional basketball player?
-- High school senior players eventually drafted by an NBA team: About three in 10,000, or 0.03 percent. That's roughly the chance of getting four of a kind in the first round of draw poker. -- High school senior players who go on to play NCAA women's basketball: About three in 100, or 3.1 percent.
