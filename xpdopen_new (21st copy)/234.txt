Chances of Dying
The odds of dying from an injury in 2014 were 1 in 1,576 according to the latest data available. The lifetime odds of dying from an injury for a person born in 2014 were 1 in 20. The odds of dying from drug poisoning were 1 in 7,586 in 2014; the lifetime odds were 1 in 96 for a person born in 2014.
