Chances of sudden death
-- Men age 40 and over have a one in eight chance of suffering sudden cardiac death, and the risk is even higher for African-American men, a study shows. For women 40 and over, the odds of suffering sudden cardiac death are one in 24, according to the study, the first to estimate lifetime risks of the condition.Nov 17, 2009
