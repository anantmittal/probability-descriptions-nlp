Between 2000-01 and 2009-10, the death rate within 30 days of admission for heart attacks almost halved, from one in 11 to one in 20. The death rate for stroke also fell, but only from one in four to one in five. About 150,000 people in Britain have a stroke each year, while it causes about 53,000 deaths annually.Mar 1, 2012
