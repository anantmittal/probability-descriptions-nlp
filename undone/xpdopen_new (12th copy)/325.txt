India has some 600,000,000 people – and about 30,000 die every year because of rabies. That gives you odds of 600,000,000:30,000 = 20,000:1 of not contracting the disease – or 1/20,000 = 50 ppm for dying from it. Folks, this is less that the risk of getting killed in traffic in the USA!
