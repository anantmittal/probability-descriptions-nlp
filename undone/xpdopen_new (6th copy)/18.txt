One absolute risk you may see is lifetime risk of breast cancer. Women in the U.S. have a "1 in 8” (or about 12 percent) lifetime risk of getting breast cancer [2]. This means for every 8 women in the U.S., 1 will be diagnosed with breast cancer during her lifetime.
