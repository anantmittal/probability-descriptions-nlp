Chances of getting pregnant
Likelihood of getting pregnant. The odds of a young fertile couple conceiving by having sexual intercourse around the time of ovulation are approximately one in five every month. Around nine out of 10 couples achieve a pregnancy after one year of unprotected sex.Aug 29, 2016
