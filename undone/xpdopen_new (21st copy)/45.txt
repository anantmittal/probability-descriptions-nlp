What is the outcome of kidney failure?
When kidney damage occurs quickly, such as when an accident occurs, it is called acute kidney failure. Those with acute kidney failure are at risk for numerous complications including: seizures, bleeding and coma. However, dialysis effectively treats those life-threatening complications.
