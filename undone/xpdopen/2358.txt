If you are dealt five cards, there are 4 × 10 = 40 possible straight flushes ( 4 × 9 = 36 if you exclude royal flushes) out of the ( 52 5 ) = 2598960 possible hands. So the probability is 40 2598960 = 1 64974 ≈ 0.00001539 … . The probability will increase if you can have more than five cards to choose from.Mar 21, 2012
