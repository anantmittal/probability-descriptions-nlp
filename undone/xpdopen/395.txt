About 10 percent to 20 percent of Americans have fatty livers. In some cases, about 2 percent to 5 percent, this condition can lead to a form of hepatitis called nonalcoholic steatohepatitis (NASH). NASH can cause cirrhosis and is another risk factor for liver cancer. You can develop liver cancer in other ways.Jun 16, 2009
