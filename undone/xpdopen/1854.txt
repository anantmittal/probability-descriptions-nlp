The odds of winning or sharing a Mega Millions jackpot (2013-2017 version): 1 in about 258.9 million. The overall odds of winning a prize were 1 in 14.71, including the base $1 prize for a "Mega Ball"-only match. Prizes and odds (2013-2017 version): 5 numbers plus the Mega Ball (5+1): 1 in 258,890,850.
