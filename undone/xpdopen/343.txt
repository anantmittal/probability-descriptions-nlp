How common is gestational diabetes during pregnancy?
Gestational diabetes mellitus (GDM), or high blood sugar during pregnancy, used to be relatively rare, occurring in about 3 percent to 4 percent of pregnancies. ... Some 70 percent to 80 percent of women diagnosed with GDM in the United States eventually develop type II diabetes.
