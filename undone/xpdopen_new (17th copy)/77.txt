What are the chances of getting pregnant on nexplanon?
It is contains no estrogen. It is considered a long term contraceptive method because it prevents pregnancy for up to 3 years. It is more than 99% effective so your chance of getting pregnant when using Nexplanon is very low when it is inserted correctly.
