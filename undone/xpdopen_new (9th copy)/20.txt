Baby born at 33 weeks Survival Rate
Technology has increased the survival rate of babies born between 32 and 34 weeks. Those born between 32 and 33 weeks have an approximate 98 percent survival rate, and those delivered between 34 and 36 weeks have more than a 99 percent chance of survival, states the March of Dimes website.Jun 13, 2017
