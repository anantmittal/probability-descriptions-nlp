If you already have a baby with Down syndrome, your risk for having another increases with each pregnancy. Up to age 40, for each pregnancy your chances of having another baby with Down syndrome is about 1 in 100 (1 percent). After age 40, the risk is based your age. Talk to a genetic counselor to understand your risk.
