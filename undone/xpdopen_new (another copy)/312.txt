Russian Roulette chances
You might think that as the game progresses your chance of dying increases. In the first round there is a 1 in 6 chance of the bullet being lined up with the barrel; in the second round the chance rises to 1 in 5, all the way through to the final sixth round where the chance is 1 in 1, or certain.Jun 2, 2012
