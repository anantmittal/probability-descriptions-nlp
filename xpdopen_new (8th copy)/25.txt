Odds Against
'Odds in favor' are odds describing the if an event will occur, while 'odds against' will describe if an event will not occur. If you are familiar with gambling, 'odds against' are what Vegas gives as odds. More on that later. For the coin flip odds in favor of a HEADS outcome is 1:1, not 50%.Sep 19, 2014
