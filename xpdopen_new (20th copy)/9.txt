Miscarriage after 20 weeks
Pregnancy loss in the second trimester can be the result of a very preterm delivery (like a spontaneous miscarriage in the second trimester) or death of the fetus (called a fetal demise). ... Once a pregnancy gets to about 20 weeks gestation, less than 0.5% will end in a fetal demise.
