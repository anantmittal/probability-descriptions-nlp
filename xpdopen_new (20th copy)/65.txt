Researchers said the overall chances of an obese man obtaining a normal body weight were 1 in 210. For women, it was 1 in 124. Those odds worsen as a person's weight increases. Men with a BMI over 40 had a 1 in 1,290 chance of becoming healthy while women in that category had a 1 in 677 chance.Jul 16, 2015
