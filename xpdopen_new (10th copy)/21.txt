The use of fertility treatments will cause the number of twin births to become higher, so they could be as high as 1 in 38 with the use of fertility drugs, to about 1 in 68 without (for all types of twins). 1 in 50 people is a fraternal twin in the USA, and 1 in 150 is an identical twin.
