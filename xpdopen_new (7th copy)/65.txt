Chances of getting pregnant with hypothyroidism
Before pregnancy. If your under-active thyroid (hypothyroidism) is untreated or under-treated then you are likely to find it more difficult to conceive. ... Once you are taking medication (levothyroxine tablets) and your thyroid hormone levels are back to normal your chances of becoming pregnant should improve dramatically ...
