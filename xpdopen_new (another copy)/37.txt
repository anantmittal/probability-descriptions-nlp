What are the odds of delivering on your due date?
The obvious answer is that you're due on your “due date,” the magic day that your doctor calculated based on a standard 40-week pregnancy. But as it happens, the odds of delivering on your due date are extremely small. Only about 1 in 30 women give birth on their due date.Jul 17, 2014
