Probability of king or diamond
The probability of picking up an ace in a 52 deck of cards is 4/52 since there are 4 aces in the deck. The odds of picking up any other card is therefore 52/52 - 4/52 = 48/52. ... The probability of picking up an ace or a king is 8/52 since in a deck of 52 cards there are 4 aces and 4 kings, which totals 8.
