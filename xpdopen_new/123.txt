False positive pregnancy
After an abortion or miscarriage, a home pregnancy test may produce a false-positive result, since the levels of hCG in the body may still be relatively high. Taking a home urine pregnancy test, following an abortion or miscarriage, can be another reason for a false-positive result.Oct 25, 2017
