Chances of getting pregnant at 42
"Your chance of conceiving at age 42 is about 9 percent per cycle," explains Dr. Pasquale Patrizio, a fertility researcher, clinician and director of the Yale Fertility Center. "By 44, it's 2 percent per cycle, and at 45, it's at most 1 percent per cycle."Jul 5, 2014
