There are more than 100 types of HPV, 30-40 of which are sexually transmitted. Of these, at least 15 are high-risk HPV strains that can cause cervical cancer. The others cause no symptoms or genital warts. Up to 80 percent of women will contract HPV in their lifetime.Oct 9, 2015
