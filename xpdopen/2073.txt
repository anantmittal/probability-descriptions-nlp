Odds of having a redheaded baby
Combining you and your husband, we get a 50% chance for Rr (r from dad, R from mom) and a 50% chance for rr (r from both parents). So there is a 50% chance for a redhead (rr) and a 50% chance for a child without red hair (Rr).Nov 14, 2012
