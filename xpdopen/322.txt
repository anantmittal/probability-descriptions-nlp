Likelihood of endometriosis
Endometriosis does not “equal” infertility. ... However, in general, it is believed that the likelihood of fertility problems increases with the severity of the disease and, as in women without endometriosis, with age. It is generally believed that 60–70% of women with endometriosis are fertile.
