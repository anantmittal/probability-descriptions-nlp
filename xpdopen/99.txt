IUD falling out
Though the risk is relatively small — happening in around 3 percent of cases, Bryant estimated — it is possible for an IUD to fall out of place. “If an IUD is going to slip out of place, it will most likely happen in the first few months of use. But it may occur later,” Planned Parenthood explains.Jan 15, 2014
