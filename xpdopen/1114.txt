Chance of inheriting celiac disease
If a mother has genes associated with celiac disease, her child has a 50 percent chance of inheriting the genes, he says. (The same is true if the father has the gene). That means a child whose mother has celiac disease has a one in 22 chance of developing the disease.Jun 10, 2009
