How common is flesh eating bacteria?
Necrotizing Fasciitis. Wounded? ... Although the media commonly calls it a “flesh-eating infection,” more than one type of bacterium can cause this rare disease. These bacteria include group A Streptococcus (group A strep), Klebsiella, Clostridium, Escherichia coli, Staphylococcus aureus, and Aeromonas hydrophila.Jul 3, 2017
