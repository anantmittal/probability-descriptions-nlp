A miscarriage after week 12 is known as a late miscarriage. After 20 weeks it is a stillbirth. 1 in 5 pregnancies end in miscarriage in Ireland (often before the mother even knows she's pregnant) but 80% of miscarriages take place in the early stages of pregnancy.
