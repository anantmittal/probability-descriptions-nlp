Risk of Recurrence
Distant recurrence (metastasis) Distant recurrence (metastasis) occurs when cancer spreads beyond the breast to other organs such as the bones, liver, lungs or brain. The risk of distant recurrence is the same for people who have lumpectomy plus radiation therapy and those who have mastectomy [13].
